WHAT IS CryveCoin ?

CryveCoin - Token

CryveCoin Token is a Security Token that proposes to share profits from the CVC platform’s various trading activities including exchange, wallet fees, and ATM transaction fees with its investors. In a radical departure from the conventional cryptocurrency exchange model, CrypTollBooth is the first exchange to allow users to buy and trade more than ten cryptocurrency tokens to and from any fiat currency around the world. This means that CTB users will be able to take part in the market without having to purchase so called “hard currency”, i.e. globally popular fiat like the US Dollar (USD), British Pound (GBP) or the Euro (EUR). Using their own locally available fiat, they will be able to pay into the CTB platform directly and engage the market without that regular bottleneck.
The CVC platform wallet will enable purchase as well as sale of those currencies to the platform at market value. Funds received from CVCcan be withdrawn directly to users’ banks, PayPal accounts, and many more.
